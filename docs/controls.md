# Controls

All bindings live in `config/gameplay.json → controls` and can be remapped
there. Defaults:

## Keyboard

| Key | Ground | Air |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `A` / `←` | Carve left | Spin left |
| `D` / `→` | Carve right | Spin right |
| `W` / `↑` | Tuck (lower drag, more speed) | Frontflip |
| `S` / `↓` | Brake / tighten carve | Backflip |
| `SPACE` | Hold to charge, release to jump | — |
| `SHIFT` | Boost (drains the boost meter) | — |
| `E` / `K` | — | Grab (hold; adds style points) |
| `C` | Cycle camera (follow → first-person → drone) | |
| `P` | Toggle photo mode | |
| `R` | Respawn at the last safe point | |
| `ESC` | Pause / resume | |

`Invert flip axis` in Settings swaps W/S flip direction.

## Gamepad

## Touch (phones & tablets — landscape only)

- **Left virtual stick** — carve (X) and tuck/brake or flip (Y).
- **JUMP** — hold to charge, release to pop.
- **BOOST / GRAB** — hold buttons on the right.
- **🎥 / ⏸** — camera & pause, top centre.
- Portrait orientation shows a "rotate your device" blocker; the Fullscreen
  button on the main menu also requests a landscape orientation lock where the
  platform allows it.

## Photo mode

`P` freezes the action: drag to aim, `W/A/S/D` to fly, `SPACE`/`E` for
up/down, `SHIFT` to fly faster, **Capture** saves a PNG.

## The feel cheat-sheet

- **Ice is fast, snow is grippy.** Watch the surface name next to the
  altimeter — black ice barely turns; powder carves on rails.
- **Charge your jumps.** A full charge roughly doubles your pop.
- **Land square.** Within ~16° of clean rotation = Perfect Landing: a score
  multiplier plus a boost refill.
- **Chain quickly.** The combo multiplier grows with every trick landed inside
  4 s of the last one, and dies with a crash.
- **Thread the rings, hit the pads.** Boost rings refill a third of the meter;
  bounce pads launch you into flip territory.
- **Grab the floating power-ups.** 🧲 Crystal Magnet auto-collects nearby
  crystals, 🛡️ Ice Shield absorbs one crash, 🔥 Nitro Surge is free full
  boost, ✖️2 doubles all points and ⏳ adds time in timed modes.
